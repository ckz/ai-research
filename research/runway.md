## Products & Technology

### Gen-4 Series (2025)
- **Gen-4** (March 2025): Most advanced model to date
  - Consistent characters, objects, environments
  - Reference images + text prompts

- **Gen 4.5** (December 2025): Latest release

  - HD video from text prompts
  - Strong physics, human motion understanding
