## Products & Features

### AI Tools
- **AI Enhance**: Improve clarity and resolution
- **AI Skin**: Perfect skin by correcting blemishes
- **Smart AI Cutout**: Separate figures, objects, animals
- **AI Remove**: Remove unwanted parts
- **AI Filter**: Create characters in different styles
- **AI Hairstyle**: Change hairstyles
- **AI Expression**: Modify expressions

### Creative Tools
- AI Collage creation
- Spot color highlighting
- Mosaic and blur effects
- Layout combinations
- Background/pattern creation
- Custom stickers and filters

### Video Features
- Video mosaic with automatic figure tracking
- Retro Clip for vintage videos
- Video Face Edit for natural retouching

## Viral AI Yearbook Feature
The app became famous for generating retro-style yearbook photos:
- Users upload series of selfies
- AI generates throwback yearbook photos
- Different poses, looks, and hairstyles
- Became #1 trending free app on App Store (2023)
