## Founders & Leadership
- **Jan Zoltowski** - Founder

### Founder Story
Jan Zoltowski launched the platform in 2023. In just the first week, it gained over one million users, demonstrating immediate product-market fit in the AI companion/roleplay space.

## Products & Features

### Core Platform
- Create, share, and interact with AI-driven roleplay characters
- User-generated character scenarios
- More permissive content policies than mainstream chatbots
- Third-party API access rather than proprietary infrastructure

### JanitorLLM
- Beta remains free
- "More flexible" with no strict message cap
- Major selling point for user acquisition

### Content Policy
- Less restrictive than Character.AI
- Allows adult-oriented conversations
- Appeals to users seeking unfiltered AI interactions

## Revenue Model

### Current Status
- Subscription model was planned for early 2025 but delayed
- Focus shifted to feature improvements
- Paid plans not yet live as of mid-2025

### Monetization Approach
- No official payouts, ads, or revenue share from platform
- Users monetize through creative services built around the tool
- Free model to drive user growth
