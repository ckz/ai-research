## Founders & Leadership
- **Mati Staniszewski** - Co-Founder & CEO
- **Piotr Dabkowski** - Co-Founder

### Founder Background
Two Polish childhood friends who grew up watching poorly-dubbed American movies, turning their frustration with dubbing quality into a $6.6 billion voice AI empire. Both are engineers who met in Poland.

## Products & Features

### Core Products
- **Text-to-Speech**: Ultra-realistic voice synthesis
- **Voice Cloning**: Clone any voice with minutes of audio
- **Eleven v3** (Jun 2025): 70+ languages, natural multi-speaker dialogue, audio tags
- **Eleven Music** (Aug 2025): AI music generation from text prompts
- **Voice Library**: Marketplace for synthetic voices

### Key Capabilities
- Multi-language support (70+ languages)
- Voice cloning with consent
- Real-time synthesis
- Dubbing and localization
- Audiobook production
- Gaming voice acting
