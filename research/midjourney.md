## Founders & Leadership
- **David Holz** - Founder & CEO

### Founder Background
- Grew up in South Florida (Fort Lauderdale)
- Father was a dentist on a sailboat in the Caribbean
- Math and physics student, ran design business in high school
- Working on applied mathematics PhD (took leave 2008)
- Year at Max Planck Institute as student researcher
- Two years at NASA Langley Research Center (LiDAR, Mars missions, atmospheric science)
- Co-founded Leap Motion in 2008 (hand motion hardware)
- Ran Leap Motion for 12 years (~100 employees)
- Leap Motion acquired by UltraHaptics in 2019 for $30M
