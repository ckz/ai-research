## Founders & Leadership
Gauth is a product of ByteDance.

**Origin Story:**
- Created by "Curry Z," a former math teacher
- Wanted to help students struggling with assignments
- 1 million downloads within first 6 months

**Corporate Structure:**
- Operates through GauthTech (Singapore subsidiary)
- Parent company: ByteDance

## Key Metrics
- **Global Users**: 300+ million
- **App Store Ranking**: #1 education app in US (Q3 2025, 6 consecutive days)

- **Demographics**: 61.3% female, primarily 18-25 age group

## Products & Features

### Core Technology
- **Photo Problem Solving**: Snap photo of problem (printed, handwritten, whiteboard)
- **Gauth GPT** (2024): Proprietary AI engine
- **Step-by-Step Solutions**: Detailed explanations within seconds

### Subject Coverage
- Mathematics (original focus)
- Physics
- Chemistry
- Biology
- Coding
- Writing
- Economics
- Social Sciences
- SAT Prep

### Human Tutor Network
- 50,000 tutors globally (US, India, Philippines)
- Handle ~5% of complex problems
- Top tutors earn up to $1,500/month
- Mix of programmers, graduates, educators
