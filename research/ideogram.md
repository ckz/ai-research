## Products & Features
- **Text-to-image generation** -- Industry-leading text rendering within generated images (~90% accuracy vs. ~30% for competitors)
- **Multiple style modes** -- Realistic, Design, 3D, Anime
- **Ideogram 3.0 (March 2025)** -- Latest model with improved quality, coherence, and style control
- **Subscription model** -- Free tier + paid plans for higher resolution, faster generation, and commercial use
- **API access** -- For developers and enterprise integration
- **Key differentiator:** Accurate text-in-image generation is the primary moat. Marketing materials, logos, posters, and branded content require readable text -- a use case where Ideogram dramatically outperforms Midjourney, DALL-E, and Stable Diffusion.

## Competitive Position
Competing with: Midjourney, OpenAI (DALL-E), Stability AI (Stable Diffusion), Adobe Firefly, Bria, KREA, Flux, and WaveSpeedAI.

Ideogram holds the #1 position for text-in-image generation. While Midjourney leads in artistic quality and DALL-E benefits from OpenAI's distribution, Ideogram owns the niche of typography-accurate image generation -- critical for commercial and marketing use cases.

## Strategic Notes
- The founding team's pedigree (Imagen, DDPM) is world-class. Jonathan Ho's DDPM paper is one of the most cited papers in generative AI history.
- Small team (49-51 people) with ~$7M revenue demonstrates capital efficiency relative to competitors burning through much larger budgets.
- Text rendering is a durable differentiator because it requires architectural innovations (not just more compute). This has allowed a 50-person team to compete with companies 10-100x their size.
- $96.5M raised is modest compared to Midjourney ($0 raised, profitable) and Stability AI ($300M+ raised). Ideogram occupies a middle ground of well-funded but disciplined.
- Key insight for startups: Ideogram demonstrates that owning a specific capability (text rendering) can be more defensible than trying to be the "best" at general image quality. Niche technical moats matter.
- Risk: As foundation models improve, text rendering may become a commodity capability, eroding Ideogram's primary differentiator. The company needs to build additional moats (enterprise workflows, brand tools, API ecosystem).

## Status
Active - Rapidly iterating on models (4 major releases in 18 months). Growing revenue and commercial adoption.
