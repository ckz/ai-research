## Products & Services
- **Slack-Native Interface**: Teams message Crosby directly in Slack
- **AI Contract Review**: Attach NDA, MSA, or sales agreement
- **Human Oversight**: Licensed attorney reviews AI output before delivery
- **Turnaround Time**: Professional legal advice typically within 1 hour

### How It Works
1. Team sends contract via Slack
2. AI agents parse document
3. AI compares clauses against market-standard terms library
4. Licensed attorney reviews, adjusts for edge cases
5. Attorney signs off before response delivered
