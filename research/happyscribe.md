## Founders & Leadership
- **Andre Bastie** - Co-Founder & CEO (French, attended Dublin City University)
- **Marc Assens Reina** - Co-Founder & CTO (Spanish)

### Founder Story
Andre co-founded Happy Scribe during his studies when he accidentally came across the challenge of transcription and built a first prototype with his flatmate and now CTO, Marc Assens Reina. The founders are Spanish and French, their company is Irish-registered, and their customers are mostly Americans.
