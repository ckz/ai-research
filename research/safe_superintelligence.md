- OpenAI co-founder and former Chief Scientist

- Voted to fire Sam Altman amid safety concerns
- Believed OpenAI prioritized commercialization over safety

### Daniel Gross
- Former head of Apple's AI efforts
- Left SSI July 2025 to join Meta Superintelligence Labs

- Investor and AI researcher
