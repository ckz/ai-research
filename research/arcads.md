# Arcads

## Overview

AI-powered UGC (User Generated Content) video ad generation platform. Creates marketing videos using AI actors in minutes instead of days.

## Founders

- **Dylan Fournier** (Co-Founder)
- **Romain Torres** (Co-Founder)

## Founding Story

Founded 2024 in France. Built platform for automated marketing video generation using AI actors. Achieved profitability within months of launch with just 7 employees.

## Products

- AI actor library (1,000+) with diverse demographics
- UGC-style video generation
- Product demo videos
- Fashion try-on videos
- Unboxing content
- 35+ language support
- 2-minute generation time
- Industry-leading lip-sync realism

## Pricing

- ~$11 per video on paid plans
- No free plan or trial (commitment required)
- Significantly cheaper than Fiverr UGC creators ($80-200/video)

## Limitations

- AI actors can't physically interact with products
- Can't show unboxing or product demonstrations
- No free trial to test quality
