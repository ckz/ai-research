## Key Metrics
- **ARR (est.):** $175M in 2022, ~$250M in 2024, projected $500M by 2026 (Sacra estimates)
- **Employees:** ~2,751 (as of January 2026)
- **Users:** 30,000+ coaches on the platform; enterprise clients include 500+ organizations
- **Key outcomes:** 52% burnout reduction; 95% user satisfaction for AI coaching; 16% increase in employee confidence
- **Growth:** Crossed $100M ARR in July 2021; 40% YoY growth through 2022

## Products & Features
- **BetterUp Coaching** -- 1:1 video sessions with certified coaches across career, leadership, mental fitness, and well-being
- **BetterUp AI Coaching (launched Jan 2025)** -- Built on 17 million data points on human growth; real-time role-specific coaching guidance
- **BetterUp Grow** -- AI-powered continuous development tool embedded in Slack and Teams; calendar-based prompts; Role Play Library with instant feedback
- **BetterUp Manage** -- AI-powered platform for leaders (launched April 2024)
- **Content Library** -- Self-guided learning resources
- **B2B Enterprise Plans** -- Custom pricing for organizations
- **B2C Individual Plans** -- $71/mo (1 session), $119/mo (2 sessions), $223/mo (4 sessions)

## Competitive Position
Competing with: Torch, CoachHub, Growthspace, Sounding Board, Bravely, and a growing wave of AI-native coaching tools.

BetterUp holds the distinction of being the largest mental health and coaching startup globally by valuation. Its hybrid human + AI approach differentiates it from pure-play AI coaching bots and from traditional coaching firms that lack technology scale.

## Strategic Notes
- The $4.7B valuation dates to October 2021 and has not been updated with a new priced round. Real-world valuation may differ significantly in the current market.
- BetterUp's "Human + AI Coaching for All" strategy (fall 2025) signals a shift from premium 1:1 coaching to broad workforce coverage via AI -- addressing the scaling bottleneck that limits all coaching companies.
- Prince Harry's involvement as Chief Impact Officer generated significant press but also scrutiny.
- Key insight for startups: BetterUp built a massive dataset (17M data points) from its human coaching sessions and is now using that proprietary data as the foundation for AI models -- a powerful flywheel that pure-AI competitors cannot easily replicate.

## Status
Active - Operating as an independent company. No new priced round since 2021 Series E. Actively shipping AI-first product updates as of fall 2025.
