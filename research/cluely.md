## Founders & Leadership
- **Chungin "Roy" Lee** - Co-Founder & CEO (age 21)
- **Neel Shanmugam** - Co-Founder & COO (age 21)
- **Alex Chen** - Co-Founder

### Founder Background
Roy Lee and Neel Shanmugam were suspended from Columbia University for developing an AI tool called "Interview Coder" to help engineers cheat on technical interviews. They pivoted this into Cluely.

## Timeline & Controversies

### Launch (April 2025)
- Launched with tagline "Cheat on Everything"
- Marketing explicitly promoted use during job interviews, exams, personal interactions
- Generated massive viral attention

### Backlash & Pivot (Late April 2025)
- Removed explicit references to cheating under scrutiny
- Founders' Columbia suspension became public

### Repositioning (November 2025)
- Rebranded as standard AI meeting assistant
- Now competes with Otter.ai and similar tools
