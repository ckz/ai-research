## Founders & Leadership

### Original Founders (departed to Google)
- **Noam Shazeer** - Co-Founder (returned to Google Sept 2024)
- **Daniel De Freitas** - Co-Founder (returned to Google Sept 2024)

### Founder Background
Both Noam Shazeer and Daniel De Freitas were former Google researchers who helped create the Transformer architecture used in popular chatbots. They left Google in 2021 to found Character.AI.

### Current Leadership
- **Karandeep Anand** - CEO (hired June 2024, former Meta and Brex executive)
