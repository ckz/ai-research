## Meta Acquisition (December 2025)

### Deal Details
- Acquisition value: $2+ billion
- Marks rare US acquisition of Asian tech company
- Largest AI bet from Meta CEO Mark Zuckerberg

### Post-Acquisition
- No continuing Chinese ownership interests
- Discontinued services and operations in China
- Meta spokesperson confirmed complete separation from China

### Geopolitical Impact
- Beijing officials surprised and displeased
- Concerns about US access to Chinese-developed technology
- Fear of template for other Chinese startups
