## Products & Features
- **Voice AI Agent Builder** — Platform to create, test, deploy, and monitor AI voice agents
- **Inbound & Outbound Calling** — Handles both customer support and outbound sales calls
- **Multi-Channel Expansion** — First solution enabling AI agents across voice, chat, email, and SMS (January 2026), positioning as complete IVR replacement
- **Retell Assure** — Automated QA for voice AI (launched December 2025), addressing the biggest operational gap in voice AI
- **Regulated Industry Support** — Healthcare, financial services, insurance, logistics, home services, retail, travel
- **Enterprise-Grade Infrastructure** — Encrypted data at rest and in transit, 99.99% uptime, global deployment capability

## Competitive Position
Competing with: Vapi, Bland AI, Synthflow, Air AI, Parloa, PolyAI, Replicant

Retell differentiates through its developer-first platform approach, compliance certifications (HIPAA, SOC 2, GDPR) for regulated industries, and extraordinary capital efficiency (profitable on $5.1M seed). The multi-channel expansion (January 2026) moves it from voice-only to full contact center replacement.

## Strategic Notes
- Capital efficiency is extraordinary — profitable with $15M+ ARR on just $5.1M in funding. This is rare in AI.
- 40M+ monthly calls demonstrates real production scale, not just a demo
- Y Combinator W24 batch + seed backing by Aaron Levie (Box CEO) and Michael Seibel (YC) = strong network
- Multi-channel expansion (voice + chat + email + SMS) is the path from voice tool to enterprise platform
- Retell Assure (QA automation) addresses a genuine pain point — enterprises need to audit AI agent quality
- Five co-founders with Big Tech pedigree (Google, Meta, ByteDance) but startup execution speed
- Voice AI market exploding: VC investment jumped from $315M (2022) to $2.1B (2024); Gartner predicts $80B in contact center labor savings by 2026
- Risk: Voice AI market is getting crowded fast; Retell's small team size means limited enterprise sales capacity; compliance gaps may slow procurement in some verticals

## Status
Active — Profitable seed-stage company with extraordinary capital efficiency ($15M+ ARR on $5.1M funding). Processing 40M+ AI calls monthly and expanding from voice-only to multi-channel enterprise platform. Likely to raise significant growth round given metrics.
