## Founders & Leadership
- **Colin Nederkoorn** - Co-Founder & CEO

### Founder Background
The founders met working at a venture-funded startup in New York City. They recognized that marketers struggled with no access to message content buried in code and customer data locked in production databases, leading them to start Customer.io.
