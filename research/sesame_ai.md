## Technology: Conversational Speech Model (CSM)

### Architecture
- Processes text and audio tokens simultaneously
- End-to-end (not speech-to-text-to-speech pipeline)
- Natural conversational flow

### Performance
- 200-300ms response times
- Human-like timing and intonation

### Human-Like Features
- Laughing
- Natural pauses
- "Um" and filler words
- Appropriate interruptions
- Emotional depth

### Open Source: CSM-1b (March 2025)
- Apache 2.0 license
- Available on GitHub (13.8K stars)
- Hugging Face (95K downloads)
