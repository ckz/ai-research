## Products & Features

### Core AI Filters
- **Age Transformation**: Make faces older/younger
- **Gender Swap**: Transform between genders
- **Smile Enhancement**: Add realistic smiles
- **Hair Color/Style**: Change hairstyles
- **Beard Addition**: Add facial hair
- **Makeup Application**: Virtual makeup

### Technology
- Neural network-based image processing
- Photorealistic transformations
- Cloud-based AI processing

## Viral Moments
- 2019: "Old Age" filter went viral globally
- Topped iOS App Store in 121 countries during 2019 surge
- Celebrity participation amplified reach

## Privacy Controversies

### Russia Concerns
- Developed by Russian company Wireless Lab
- July 2019: FBI and FTC review requested in US
- FBI flagged as potential counterintelligence risk
- Company moved from St. Petersburg to Skolkovo (2018)
- Incorporated FaceApp Technology Limited in Cyprus (June 2020)

### Company Response
- "User data is not transferred to Russia"
- Data processing occurs on cloud servers
- Added corporate presence in Cyprus and US
