# Moveworks

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|

## Key Metrics
- **ARR:** Surpassed $100M in September 2024; projected ~$142M in 2025
- **Employees:** 500+ AI experts
- **Customers:** 350+ large enterprises, 5M+ employees served, 10% of Fortune 500
- **Autonomous Resolution Rate:** 75-85% of IT support issues resolved without human intervention
- **Acquisition Price:** $2.85B (cash and stock, ServiceNow)
- **Acquisition Completed:** December 2025

## Products & Features
- **AI Assistant:** Front-end AI chatbot that resolves employee IT, HR, finance, and facilities requests autonomously using natural language.
- **Enterprise Search:** Unified search across enterprise knowledge bases and systems.
- **Autonomous Resolution:** 75-85% of support requests resolved without human escalation.
- **Multi-System Integration:** Connects to existing enterprise systems (ServiceNow, Jira, Okta, Workday, etc.) to take action on behalf of employees.
- **Multi-Department Support:** Expanded beyond IT to HR, finance, and facilities management.
- **Continuous Learning:** AI models improve over time based on resolution patterns and employee feedback.

## Competitive Position
Competed with: ServiceNow (now parent), Espressive, Freshworks, Zendesk, Aisera, Rezolve.ai.

Moveworks was recognized as a Challenger in the 2025 Gartner Magic Quadrant for AI Applications in IT Service Management. The company differentiated through its broad enterprise system integration and high autonomous resolution rates.

**Key Customers:** Unilever, Toyota, Siemens, Broadcom, CVS Health, and other Fortune 500 companies.

## Strategic Notes
- The $2.85B acquisition by ServiceNow represents the largest purchase of a private, venture-backed tech company in 2025 (per Crunchbase).
- Acquisition provided a strong return for early investors -- Lightspeed and Bain Capital participated from Series A onward.
- ServiceNow acquired Moveworks specifically for the AI agent market, framing the deal around the "burgeoning market for AI agents that can perform digital tasks for people."
- The majority of Moveworks' customer deployments already used ServiceNow as a system of action, making the integration natural.
- Post-acquisition, Moveworks' 500+ AI team will strengthen ServiceNow's agentic AI capabilities.
- Revenue trajectory projected to reach $564M by 2030 (MVR estimates).
- The AI agent market grew from $5.25B (2024) to $7.84B (2025), projected to reach $52.62B by 2030.

## Status
Acquired -- ServiceNow completed the acquisition of Moveworks for $2.85B in December 2025. The company now operates as part of ServiceNow's AI platform, extending agentic AI capabilities for enterprise employee support.
