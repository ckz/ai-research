## Products & Features

### Editing Tools
- Frame-accurate timeline
- Clip-level editing
- Auto-enhance features
- Green screen effects
- Transitions
- 4K export (no watermark)
- Clips up to 10 minutes
- Draft management

### Audio Features
- Enhanced audio (clearer voices)
- Background noise removal
- Automatic caption generation
- Customizable captions
- 150+ sound effects (Dec 2025 update)

### AI-Powered Features
- **SAM 3 Model**: Meta's Segment Anything Model
- AI effects on specific people/objects
- Scribble, Outline, Glitter effects
- Object tagging and blurring
- AI animation (bring images to life)
- AI segmentation of objects

### Analytics & Insights
- Real-time distribution feedback
- Skip rate tracking
- Live insights dashboard
- Engagement understanding

### December 2025 Update
- AI-powered effects (SAM model)
- New title options
- New Year's font styles
- Font style presets
- Word-specific caption styling
