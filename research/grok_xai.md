## Products

### Grok Chatbot
- Launched November 2023
- Modeled after "The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy"
- Real-time information access from X platform
- Known for wit, sarcasm, and personality
- Less content restrictions than competitors

### Grok 4
- Latest model version
- 8 million daily users
- Fast variant (Grok 4 Fast) for quicker responses

### Infrastructure
- Memphis supercomputer (largest in the world per Musk)
- 200,000 GPUs, scaling to 1 million by 2026
- Built in 122 days
