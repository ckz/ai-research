## Products & Features (Pre-Acquisition)

### Facemoji SDK
- Plug-and-play avatar system for developers
- Add avatars with few lines of code
- Lightweight proprietary rendering engine
- Custom ML pipeline (developed in-house)
- Doesn't require Unity plugins
- Full avatar art system out-of-box

### Technical Capabilities
- Computer vision
- 3D technology
- Machine learning
- Rendering engines
- Web3/blockchain support
- NFT integration

## Key Metrics (Pre-Acquisition)
- **Users**: 1+ million (in testing)
- **SDK Model**: Free for developers

## Google Acquisition (2022)

### Deal Details
- Google quietly acquired Alter (formerly Facemoji) in late 2022
- Reported acquisition price: ~$100 million
- Strategic move to compete with TikTok on content
- Acquired to boost YouTube/content offerings

### Post-Acquisition
- Team integrated into Google
- Technology likely used for:
  - YouTube avatar features
  - Google Meet enhancements
  - Other Google products

## Strategic Notes (Historical)
- Twitter investment showed social platform interest
- SDK approach (B2B) rather than consumer app
- Lightweight solution differentiated from Unity-dependent competitors
- Gaming and social media use cases
- Web3/NFT features aligned with 2021-22 trends

## Current Status
**Acquired** - Now part of Google (2022)

Product development continues under Google ownership, likely integrated into Google's consumer products.
