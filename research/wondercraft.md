## Products & Features
- **AI Audio Studio** -- Full production suite: AI script writing, voice generation, music, sound effects, editing
- **Templates** -- Convert blogs to podcasts, articles to audiobooks, ad copy to audio campaigns
- **Ultra-realistic AI voices** -- Powered by ElevenLabs technology; custom voice cloning available
- **Translation & dubbing** -- 15+ languages with team of language experts for quality assurance
- **Video generation** -- AI-generated video from audio content
- **Collaboration tools** -- Multi-user editing and production workflows
- **Key differentiator:** End-to-end audio production (not just voice synthesis) with professional-grade templates and workflow, plus strategic ElevenLabs partnership for best-in-class voice quality

## Competitive Position
Competing with: Podcastle, Suno, Udio, Descript, Spotify for Podcasters, Riverside.fm, and a growing wave of AI audio generation tools.

Wondercraft differentiates as a full-stack audio production platform (writing + voice + music + editing + distribution) rather than a single-feature voice generator. The "Canva of audio" positioning targets the massive market of non-audio-professionals who want to create quality content.

## Strategic Notes
- The ElevenLabs backing is strategically significant: it provides access to best-in-class voice synthesis technology while signaling that ElevenLabs views Wondercraft as a complementary application layer, not a competitor.
- Y Combinator pedigree + Steven Bartlett backing (one of the world's most popular podcasters) creates distribution advantages.
- The audio content market is exploding: 500M+ podcast listeners globally, $4B+ podcast advertising market.
- Key insight for startups: Wondercraft exemplifies the "application layer on top of foundation models" strategy -- using ElevenLabs voices + LLMs for scripting + their own production UX to create a defensible product without training their own models.
- At $3.5M raised and 8 employees, Wondercraft is very early-stage. The question is whether they can scale distribution and enterprise adoption before better-funded competitors (Descript, etc.) add similar features.

## Status
Active - Seed-stage company growing user base. Expanding platform features and language support.
