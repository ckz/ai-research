## About Google Labs
Google Labs is Google's home for AI experiments, serving as a public testing ground for innovative AI-powered features before they potentially become mainstream products. It allows users to explore early-stage AI technology and provide feedback.

## Key Experiments & Products (2025)

### CC (Gmail AI Agent)
- Experimental AI productivity agent in Gmail
- Provides personalized email briefing every morning
- Users can email CC anytime for help

### Disco & GenTabs
- New browsing experience for complex online tasks
- Built with Gemini 3
- Creates interactive web applications based on open tabs and chat history
- Example: Planning a Japan trip generates interactive maps and itinerary builders
- Essentially "vibe coding" built into web browsing

### Doppl
- Fashion discovery and virtual try-on app
- December 2025 updates added Fashion Discovery Feed
- Direct shopping features integrated

### Pomelli
- AI experiment for small-to-medium-sized businesses
- Generates scalable, on-brand social media campaigns
- Analyzes websites to learn brand identity
- Generates tailored campaign ideas and marketing assets

### Learn Your Way
- Powered by LearnLM (Google's learning-focused models)
- Transforms static textbooks into active learning experiences
- Interactive quizzes with real-time assessment
- Efficacy study: Students scored 11 percentage points higher on retention tests

### Whisk
- Image-based prompting tool
- Drag and drop three images: Subject, Scene, Style
- Gemini writes captions automatically
- Imagen 3 creates the blend

### Mixboard
- Visual mood board creation tool
- Real-time collaboration
- Now available in 180+ countries
- Powered by Nano Banana Pro
- Can turn boards into presentation slides

## Underlying Technology

- Improvements in reasoning, multimodality, and efficiency
- Creative tools: Flow, Music AI Sandbox
- Generative media models: Veo 3.1, Imagen 4, Flow
