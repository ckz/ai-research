## Target Users
Neurodivergent individuals:
- ADHD
- Autism
- Executive dysfunction
- Anyone who finds tasks overwhelming

## Products & Features

### Magic To Do (Flagship)
- Automatically breaks down tasks into smaller steps
- Helps manage and organize to-do lists
- Makes overwhelming tasks manageable

### Tone Check
- Analyzes message tone
- Helps rewrite to fit desired tone
- Assists with social communication

### Time Estimation
- Provides objective time estimates
- Helps with time blindness
- Planning assistance

### Decision Support
- Helps make decisions
- Turns vague goals into actionable steps
- Reduces decision paralysis
