### Kling O1 (December 2025)
- Industry's first unified multimodal creation tool
- Integrates text, video, image, and subject inputs
- Consolidates generation and editing into single engine
- Next-generation video and imaging architectures

### Kling Video 2.6 (December 2025)
- Milestone "simultaneous audio-visual generation"
- Transforms traditional AI video production workflow

## Technical Architecture
- **Foundation**: Diffusion-based transformer (DiT)
- **Enhancement**: Self-developed 3D variational autoencoder (VAE)
- **Capability**: Synchronous spatiotemporal compression
- **Output**: Up to 1080p HD video

## Key Features
- **Text-to-Video**: Generate videos from text prompts
- **Image-to-Video**: Transform images into video
- **Multi-Image Reference**: Maintain visual consistency across frames
- **DeepSeek Integration** (Mar 2025): AI-assisted prompt generation

## Integration
- DeepSeek-R1 integration for prompt assistance
- Lowers entry barrier for AI video creation
- Available via KuaiYing video editing app
