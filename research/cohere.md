## Founders & Leadership
- **Aidan Gomez** -- Co-Founder & CEO. British-Canadian computer scientist. At age 20, as an intern at Google Brain in 2017, co-authored "Attention Is All You Need" -- the foundational Transformer paper that underpins modern AI. BS from the University of Toronto. One of the most prominent young founders in AI.
- **Ivan Zhang** -- Co-Founder. Former Google researcher.
- **Nick Frosst** -- Co-Founder. Former Google researcher.
- **Joelle Pineau** -- Chief AI Officer (hired August 2025). Previously VP of AI at Meta. World-renowned AI researcher.
- **Francois Chadwick** -- CFO (hired August 2025). Previously took Uber public -- hire signals IPO preparation.

## Key Metrics
- **ARR:** $240M (2025), surpassing $200M target; crossed $100M annualized by May 2025
- **Revenue Growth:** 50%+ quarter-over-quarter throughout 2025; tripled from $35M run rate (March 2024) to $100M+ (May 2025)
- **Employees:** 837 (January 2026), up from ~300 (2024)
- **Gross Margins:** ~70% (2025), expanding 25 basis points YoY
- **Offices:** Toronto (HQ), San Francisco, Montreal, London, New York City, Paris, Seoul
- **IPO Target:** H2 2026 (potential dual listing on TSX and NASDAQ)

## Products & Features
- **Command Family:** LLMs designed for efficient deployment on limited GPU infrastructure, addressing enterprise cost and performance concerns.
- **North Platform:** Turnkey AI platform for building secure AI agents and enterprise workflows. Launched 2025.
- **Sovereign AI Deployment:** Models can run on-premises, in private clouds, or through managed cloud services -- a key differentiator for governments and regulated industries.
- **Multilingual AI:** Strong multilingual capabilities across 100+ languages, critical for global enterprise deployment.
- **Embed & Rerank:** Industry-leading retrieval models for RAG architectures.
- **Enterprise Security:** Designed for regulated industries with private deployment options and data sovereignty guarantees.

## Competitive Position
Competing with: OpenAI, Anthropic, Google (Gemini), Microsoft (Azure OpenAI), Meta (Llama), Mistral AI, AI21 Labs.

Cohere differentiates through three key pillars: (1) sovereign/private deployment -- enterprises and governments can run Cohere models entirely on their own infrastructure; (2) multilingual capabilities -- strong performance across 100+ languages; (3) capital efficiency -- focused on enterprise-specific use cases rather than brute-force scaling.

## Strategic Notes
- The IPO preparation (CFO who took Uber public + Chief AI Officer from Meta + regulatory filings) is the clearest signal in the AI startup ecosystem of a near-term public offering.
- $240M ARR with 50%+ QoQ growth and 70% gross margins suggests a strong IPO candidate. At 15-25x ARR (typical AI company multiples), a public valuation could range from $3.6B to $6B -- potentially a down-round from private valuation.
- The sovereign AI angle is increasingly valuable as countries (Canada, EU, Middle East) invest in domestic AI capabilities.
- The "Attention Is All You Need" co-authorship gives Aidan Gomez unique credibility -- he literally co-invented the technology that powers modern AI.
- Cohere's capital-efficient approach ($1.5B raised vs. OpenAI's $10B+) may be attractive to public market investors focused on path to profitability.
- Employee count nearly tripled from ~300 to 837 between 2024 and early 2026, reflecting rapid scaling.
- Acquisition of Ottogrid (May 2025) adds market research automation capabilities.
- Key risk: OpenAI and Anthropic are aggressively expanding in enterprise; Cohere must prove its specialized approach wins against larger, better-funded rivals.
- Dual TSX/NASDAQ listing would make Cohere one of Canada's most prominent AI IPOs.

## Status
Active -- Late-stage private company with $6.8B-$7B valuation, $240M ARR, and active IPO preparation targeting H2 2026. The leading candidate for the first major AI foundation model company IPO.
