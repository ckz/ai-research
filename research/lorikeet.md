## Founders & Leadership
- **Steve Hind** - Co-Founder & CEO (ex-Stripe product lead)
- **Jamie Hall** - Co-Founder (ex-Google Brain researcher)

### Founder Backgrounds
**Steve Hind:**
- Led product teams at Stripe and Watershed
- Built tooling for complex processes like carbon accounting and financial reporting at scale

**Jamie Hall:**
- Research tech lead at Google Brain
- Led research on factual grounding in large language models
- Third named author on Google's breakthrough 2022 LaMDA paper
- Fourth named author on the 2020 Meena paper
