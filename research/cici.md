## Key Metrics

- **Languages Supported**: 18

## Products & Features
- Text generation (using OpenAI GPT and Google Gemini)
- Voice input and interaction
- Image generation
- Personalized AI agents
- Math problem-solving (highlighted in marketing)
- Free to use

## Regional Availability
- **Available**: UK, Mexico, Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines, Southeast Asia
- **Not Available**: China (Doubao serves this market), United States
- **Region-locked**: Uses GPT and Gemini instead of ByteDance proprietary models

## Market Performance (2025)
- #1 most downloaded free app in Mexico (Oct 2025, full week)
- Top 20 rankings in UK, Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines for 3+ consecutive months
- 400+ different advertisements run in Mexico alone (Oct 2025)

## Strategic Notes
- Stealth marketing approach - ByteDance barely mentioned in app or materials
- International version of Doubao
- Uses third-party AI models (OpenAI, Google) instead of proprietary tech
- Heavy advertising spend, especially in Latin America
- Free usage as key competitive advantage
